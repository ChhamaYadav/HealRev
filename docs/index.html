Our Process

How It Works

From day one to ongoing collections — a seamless, fully managed process so you can focus on patients.

01
🤝

Onboard & Credential

We collect your practice details, complete provider credentialing with payers, and configure billing workflows — typically in under 2 weeks.

02
✅

Verify & Capture

Real-time eligibility checks before every encounter. Charges are captured accurately with correct ICD-10 and CPT codes — first time, every time.

03
📤

Submit & Follow Up

Claims are submitted within 24 hours of service. Denials are worked proactively — not reactively. Every claim is tracked to resolution.

04
💰

Collect & Report

Payments are posted and reconciled. You receive clear, actionable financial dashboards — so you always know exactly where your revenue stands.
